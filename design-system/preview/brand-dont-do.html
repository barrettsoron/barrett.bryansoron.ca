Avoid

  • Gradients, glassmorphism, AI sparkles
  • Emoji as iconography
  • "Click here", "Stay tuned", "Game-changer"
  • Rounded soft cards with coloured left rule
  • Sentence-end exclamation marks

Reach for

  • One column, generous space, hairline rules
  • Sentence case, em-dashes, ISO dates
  • § folio between sections
  • Mono uppercase eyebrows on labels
  • Accent green as a budget, not a palette
