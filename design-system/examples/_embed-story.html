Fastmail, not Proton.

An essay in six cards · May 2026

@barrett 01 / 06

The setup

I switched mail providers last year, and a lot of people asked me why I didn't choose Proton.

The short version: privacy is a means, not the end. The end is doing what I want with my own correspondence, for as long as I want to.

Proton optimises for the first. Fastmail optimises for the second.

@barrett 02 / 06

A position

Encryption that I cannot read with a standard mail client in twenty years is not, for me, durability.

The trade-off

I traded end-to-end encryption for IMAP, JMAP, and a thirty-year migration path.

I keep the same inbox in mutt, in Mail.app, in a hand-rolled Python script, and in whatever client exists in 2046.
